üìä Assessment

Formative Assessment:

  • Quality of geographic reasoning and analysis
  • Effective use of evidence from multiple sources
  • Application of advanced writing techniques
  • Participation in peer feedback and collaboration

Summative Assessment:

  • Advanced writing rubric focusing on:
    • Sophisticated geographic argumentation (25%)
    • Synthesis of multiple sources and perspectives (25%)
    • Advanced academic vocabulary and terminology (20%)
    • Connection to Alaska context and global patterns (15%)
    • Writing clarity, organization, and mechanics (15%)

Alaska Cultural Competency:

  • Respectful integration of Alaska Native perspectives
  • Understanding of Alaska's place in global geography
  • Application of geographic concepts to Alaska challenges and opportunities

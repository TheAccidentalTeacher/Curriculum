üìä Comprehensive Assessment Framework

üéØ Standards-Based Assessment Rubric

üèõÔ∏è Historical Synthesis (Alaska History Standards)

Exemplary (4): Demonstrates sophisticated understanding of historical patterns, expertly connects past to present, synthesizes multiple perspectives

Proficient (3): Shows solid historical understanding, connects past to present effectively, synthesizes most perspectives

Developing (2): Shows basic historical understanding, makes some connections, synthesizes some perspectives

Beginning (1): Shows minimal historical understanding, limited connections, needs support synthesizing perspectives

Beginning (1): Communicates with support, constructs minimal arguments, limited audience adaptation

Beginning (1): Designs minimal solutions, applies few scientific principles, needs support for innovation

üåç Global Citizenship Development

Exemplary (4): Demonstrates exceptional global citizenship understanding, commits deeply to action, inspires others

Proficient (3): Demonstrates solid global citizenship understanding, commits to action, collaborates effectively

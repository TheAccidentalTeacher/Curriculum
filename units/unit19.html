Unit Overview

This unit examines different economic systems, global trade patterns, and economic development strategies used by countries around the world.

6th Grade Lessons üè™

  1. Lesson 1: Basic Economic Systems

    • Objective: Understand the three basic economic systems.
    • Content: Traditional, command, and market economies with examples.
    • Activities: Economic system comparison chart, country classification.
    • Assessment: Economic systems quiz.

  2. Lesson 2: Natural Resources and Economy

    • Objective: Explore how natural resources affect economic development.
    • Content: Renewable and non-renewable resources, resource distribution, economic impact.
    • Activities: Resource mapping, Alaska resource study.
    • Assessment: Natural resources and economy report.

  3. Lesson 3: Trade and Commerce

    • Objective: Learn about international trade and its importance.
    • Content: Imports, exports, trade routes, specialization, interdependence.
    • Activities: Trade route mapping, product origin tracking.
    • Assessment: International trade presentation.

  4. Lesson 4: Economic Development Levels

    • Objective: Compare developed, developing, and least developed countries.
    • Content: GDP, standard of living, development indicators, quality of life.
    • Activities: Development level mapping, indicator comparison.
    • Assessment: Development levels poster.

7th Grade Lessons üè≠

  1. Lesson 1: Globalization and Economic Integration

    • Objective: Analyze the process and effects of economic globalization.
    • Content: Multinational corporations, global supply chains, free trade agreements.
    • Activities: Supply chain tracking, globalization impact study.
    • Assessment: Globalization effects analysis.

  2. Lesson 2: Development Strategies

    • Objective: Investigate different approaches to economic development.
    • Content: Import substitution, export promotion, sustainable development.
    • Activities: Development strategy comparison, case study analysis.
    • Assessment: Development strategy evaluation.

  3. Lesson 3: Economic Inequality

    • Objective: Examine income inequality within and between countries.
    • Content: Wealth distribution, poverty, social mobility, inequality measures.
    • Activities: Inequality data analysis, poverty mapping.
    • Assessment: Economic inequality report.

  4. Lesson 4: Technology and Economic Change

    • Objective: Explore how technology transforms economies.
    • Content: Automation, digital economy, technological leapfrogging.
    • Activities: Technology impact study, digital divide analysis.
    • Assessment: Technology and economy project.

8th Grade Lessons üìä

  1. Lesson 1: International Financial Systems

    • Objective: Analyze global financial institutions and monetary systems.
    • Content: World Bank, IMF, currency systems, financial crises.
    • Activities: Financial institution study, crisis case analysis.
    • Assessment: Global finance evaluation.

  2. Lesson 2: Sustainable Economic Development

    • Objective: Investigate sustainable approaches to economic growth.
    • Content: Green economy, circular economy, environmental economics.
    • Activities: Sustainability model design, green economy analysis.
    • Assessment: Sustainable development framework.

  3. Lesson 3: Economic Blocs and Regional Integration

    • Objective: Examine regional economic cooperation and integration.
    • Content: EU, NAFTA/USMCA, ASEAN, regional trade agreements.
    • Activities: Economic bloc comparison, integration benefits analysis.
    • Assessment: Regional integration evaluation.

  4. Lesson 4: Future Economic Challenges

    • Objective: Evaluate emerging economic challenges and opportunities.
    • Content: Aging populations, climate change economics, space economy.
    • Activities: Future challenges assessment, opportunity identification.
    • Assessment: Future economy strategy proposal.

Unit Assessment

  • 6th Grade: Economic systems quiz, resources report, trade presentation, development poster.
  • 7th Grade: Globalization analysis, strategy evaluation, inequality report, technology project.
  • 8th Grade: Global finance evaluation, sustainability framework, integration evaluation, future strategy.

Lesson 1: Systems Thinking in Sustainability

Objective: Students will apply systems thinking to understand complex sustainability challenges.

üîÑ Systems Approach:

  • Interconnectedness: How environmental, social, and economic systems connect
  • Feedback Loops: How changes in one part affect the whole system
  • Emergent Properties: System behaviors that arise from interactions
  • Leverage Points: Places where small changes create big impacts
  • Unintended Consequences: Unexpected results of interventions
  • Adaptive Management: Learning and adjusting strategies over time

üìä Environmental Assessment Tools:

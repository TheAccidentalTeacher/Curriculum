Unit Overview

This unit explores South America's diverse landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and economic development patterns, emphasizing environmental challenges and regional cooperation.

6th Grade Lessons üèîÔ∏è

  1. Lesson 1: Physical Geography

    • Objective: Identify South America's major physical features and countries.
    • Content: Andes Mountains, Amazon River, Brazilian Highlands, Pampas, 12 countries.
    • Activities: Physical features mapping, country identification game.
    • Assessment: Geography quiz and landform diagram.

  2. Lesson 2: Amazon Rainforest

    • Objective: Explore the Amazon's importance to global environment.
    • Content: Biodiversity, indigenous peoples, deforestation threats.
    • Activities: Rainforest ecosystem model, conservation poster.
    • Assessment: Amazon importance presentation.

  3. Lesson 3: Cultural Heritage

    • Objective: Learn about South America's diverse cultures and languages.
    • Content: Inca civilization, Spanish/Portuguese colonization, modern cultures.
    • Activities: Cultural comparison chart, Inca achievement timeline.
    • Assessment: Cultural diversity project.

  4. Lesson 4: Economic Activities

    • Objective: Understand South America's main economic activities.
    • Content: Agriculture, mining, manufacturing, tourism.
    • Activities: Economic activity mapping, country specialization research.
    • Assessment: Economic activities poster.

7th Grade Lessons üåø

  1. Lesson 1: Environmental Geography

    • Objective: Analyze environmental challenges and conservation efforts.
    • Content: Deforestation, mining impacts, climate change, protected areas.
    • Activities: Environmental impact study, conservation strategy design.
    • Assessment: Environmental management plan.

  2. Lesson 2: Indigenous Rights and Land

    • Objective: Examine indigenous peoples' struggles and rights.
    • Content: Land rights, cultural preservation, modern challenges.
    • Activities: Indigenous rights case study, cultural preservation project.
    • Assessment: Rights advocacy presentation.

  3. Lesson 3: Urbanization and Development

    • Objective: Investigate rapid urbanization and its consequences.
    • Content: Megacities, favelas, urban planning, inequality.
    • Activities: Urban problem analysis, city planning project.
    • Assessment: Urban development proposal.

  4. Lesson 4: Regional Economic Integration

    • Objective: Explore economic cooperation and trade relationships.
    • Content: Mercosur, UNASUR, trade patterns, economic challenges.
    • Activities: Trade analysis, regional cooperation timeline.
    • Assessment: Economic integration report.

8th Grade Lessons ‚ö°

  1. Lesson 1: Geopolitical Dynamics

    • Objective: Analyze political changes and regional power dynamics.
    • Content: Democratic transitions, authoritarian periods, regional leadership.
    • Activities: Political timeline analysis, democracy assessment.
    • Assessment: Political development evaluation.

  2. Lesson 2: Resource Extraction and Global Markets

    • Objective: Evaluate the impact of resource dependence on development.
    • Content: Mining, oil, agriculture, commodity price volatility.
    • Activities: Resource curse analysis, diversification strategies.
    • Assessment: Economic diversification plan.

  3. Lesson 3: Climate Change and Adaptation

    • Objective: Investigate climate change impacts and adaptation strategies.
    • Content: Glacier retreat, drought, flooding, agricultural shifts.
    • Activities: Climate impact mapping, adaptation planning.
    • Assessment: Climate adaptation strategy.

  4. Lesson 4: Social Movements and Change

    • Objective: Examine social movements and their impact on society.
    • Content: Human rights movements, environmental activism, women's rights.
    • Activities: Social movement case study, activism timeline.
    • Assessment: Social change analysis.

Unit Assessment

  • 6th Grade: Geography quiz, Amazon presentation, cultural project, economic poster.
  • 7th Grade: Environmental plan, rights presentation, urban proposal, integration report.
  • 8th Grade: Political evaluation, diversification plan, adaptation strategy, social change analysis.

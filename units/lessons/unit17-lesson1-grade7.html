Analysis Questions:

  • How does this map differ from modern Alaska maps?

[Primary source: Traditional Inupiat place names and their meanings]

Analysis Questions:

  • How do traditional place names describe geographic features?
  • What does this reveal about indigenous geographic knowledge?
  • How is this different from European mapping traditions?

Unit Overview

This unit examines global environmental challenges, their geographic distribution, causes, and international efforts to address climate change and environmental degradation.

6th Grade Lessons üå±

  1. Lesson 1: Types of Environmental Problems

    • Objective: Identify major environmental issues around the world.
    • Content: Air pollution, water pollution, deforestation, habitat loss.
    • Activities: Environmental problems mapping, issue identification.
    • Assessment: Environmental issues poster.

  2. Lesson 2: Climate Change Basics

    • Objective: Understand what climate change is and its causes.
    • Content: Greenhouse effect, global warming, human activities, evidence.
    • Activities: Climate change evidence study, greenhouse effect model.
    • Assessment: Climate change explanation presentation.

  3. Lesson 3: Environmental Protection Efforts

    • Objective: Learn about conservation and protection programs.
    • Content: National parks, wildlife reserves, recycling, renewable energy.
    • Activities: Conservation project design, protection success stories.
    • Assessment: Environmental protection plan.

  4. Lesson 4: Alaska's Environmental Challenges

    • Objective: Examine environmental issues specific to Alaska.
    • Content: Arctic ice loss, permafrost thaw, wildlife impacts, adaptation.
    • Activities: Alaska environmental change study, adaptation strategies.
    • Assessment: Alaska environmental report.

7th Grade Lessons üî•

  1. Lesson 1: Climate Change Impacts by Region

    • Objective: Analyze how climate change affects different world regions.
    • Content: Sea level rise, drought, extreme weather, regional vulnerability.
    • Activities: Regional impact mapping, vulnerability assessment.
    • Assessment: Climate impact analysis.

  2. Lesson 2: Environmental Justice and Inequality

    • Objective: Investigate how environmental problems affect different populations.
    • Content: Environmental racism, pollution exposure, resource access.
    • Activities: Environmental justice case study, inequality mapping.
    • Assessment: Environmental justice report.

  3. Lesson 3: International Environmental Agreements

    • Objective: Explore global cooperation on environmental issues.
    • Content: Paris Agreement, Kyoto Protocol, international negotiations.
    • Activities: Agreement timeline, negotiation simulation.
    • Assessment: International cooperation evaluation.

  4. Lesson 4: Sustainable Development Solutions

    • Objective: Investigate sustainable development approaches.
    • Content: Renewable energy, green technology, sustainable cities.
    • Activities: Sustainability project design, technology assessment.
    • Assessment: Sustainable development proposal.

8th Grade Lessons ‚ôªÔ∏è

  1. Lesson 1: Environmental Policy and Economics

    • Objective: Analyze the economics of environmental policy and regulation.
    • Content: Carbon pricing, environmental regulations, cost-benefit analysis.
    • Activities: Policy analysis, economic modeling.
    • Assessment: Environmental policy evaluation.

  2. Lesson 2: Technology and Environmental Solutions

    • Objective: Investigate technological approaches to environmental challenges.
    • Content: Clean technology, geoengineering, innovation strategies.
    • Activities: Technology assessment, innovation timeline.
    • Assessment: Technology solution analysis.

  3. Lesson 3: Environmental Activism and Social Movements

    • Objective: Examine environmental movements and their effectiveness.
    • Content: Environmental activism, grassroots movements, policy influence.
    • Activities: Movement case study, activism timeline.
    • Assessment: Environmental movement analysis.

  4. Lesson 4: Future Environmental Scenarios

    • Objective: Evaluate potential future environmental outcomes and responses.
    • Content: Climate scenarios, adaptation strategies, resilience planning.
    • Activities: Scenario planning, resilience assessment.
    • Assessment: Environmental future strategy.

Unit Assessment

  • 6th Grade: Environmental issues poster, climate change presentation, protection plan, Alaska report.
  • 7th Grade: Climate impact analysis, environmental justice report, cooperation evaluation, development proposal.
  • 8th Grade: Environmental policy evaluation, technology analysis, movement analysis, future strategy.

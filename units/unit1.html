Unit Overview

This unit introduces students to the field of geography, its branches, and the tools geographers use to study the world. Students will explore the five themes of geography and learn how to analyze the world from a geographer's perspective.

    • Assessment: Share one thing you learned about geography.

Unit Assessment

  • 8th Grade: Argumentative essay, digital portfolio, group presentation.

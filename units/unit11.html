Unit Overview

This unit explores Russia's vast territory and Central Asia's strategic location, emphasizing their shared Soviet history and current geopolitical importance.

6th Grade Lessons ‚ùÑÔ∏è

  1. Lesson 1: Geography of Russia

    • Objective: Identify Russia's massive size and major geographic features.
    • Content: 11 time zones, Siberia, Ural Mountains, major rivers.
    • Activities: Size comparison project, time zone calculation.
    • Assessment: Russia geography quiz.

  2. Lesson 2: Climate and Environment

    • Objective: Understand Russia's harsh climate and its effects.
    • Content: Tundra, taiga, continental climate, permafrost.
    • Activities: Climate zone mapping, permafrost experiment.
    • Assessment: Climate impact diagram.

  3. Lesson 3: Central Asian Countries

    • Objective: Learn about the five Central Asian republics.
    • Content: Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan.
    • Activities: Country profile creation, cultural comparison.
    • Assessment: Central Asia fact file.

  4. Lesson 4: Natural Resources

    • Objective: Explore the region's abundant natural resources.
    • Content: Oil, natural gas, minerals, forests, farmland.
    • Activities: Resource mapping, trade relationship study.
    • Assessment: Resource importance presentation.

7th Grade Lessons üèõÔ∏è

  1. Lesson 1: Soviet Legacy

    • Objective: Analyze the lasting impact of the Soviet Union.
    • Content: Soviet system, planned economy, ethnic policies, infrastructure.
    • Activities: Soviet impact analysis, before/after comparison.
    • Assessment: Soviet legacy report.

  2. Lesson 2: Post-Soviet Transitions

    • Objective: Investigate different paths taken after Soviet collapse.
    • Content: Economic transitions, political systems, regional cooperation.
    • Activities: Transition comparison, success factors analysis.
    • Assessment: Transition evaluation project.

  3. Lesson 3: Energy Politics

    • Objective: Examine the role of energy in regional politics.
    • Content: Pipeline politics, energy diplomacy, resource dependence.
    • Activities: Energy infrastructure mapping, diplomacy simulation.
    • Assessment: Energy politics analysis.

  4. Lesson 4: Environmental Challenges

    • Objective: Investigate environmental problems and solutions.
    • Content: Aral Sea crisis, industrial pollution, climate change impacts.
    • Activities: Environmental case study, solution brainstorming.
    • Assessment: Environmental action plan.

8th Grade Lessons üåç

  1. Lesson 1: Geopolitical Strategy

    • Objective: Analyze Russia's global strategic objectives and methods.
    • Content: Sphere of influence, great power competition, military projection.
    • Activities: Strategic analysis, power projection mapping.
    • Assessment: Geopolitical strategy evaluation.

  2. Lesson 2: Economic Development Models

    • Objective: Compare different economic development approaches in the region.
    • Content: State capitalism, resource economies, diversification efforts.
    • Activities: Economic model comparison, development strategy analysis.
    • Assessment: Development model evaluation.

  3. Lesson 3: Cultural and Religious Dynamics

    • Objective: Investigate cultural and religious diversity and tensions.
    • Content: Orthodox Christianity, Islam, ethnic minorities, cultural policies.
    • Activities: Cultural mapping, religious freedom analysis.
    • Assessment: Cultural dynamics report.

  4. Lesson 4: Regional Security Issues

    • Objective: Examine security challenges and cooperation efforts.
    • Content: Border conflicts, terrorism, organized crime, security cooperation.
    • Activities: Security challenge mapping, cooperation analysis.
    • Assessment: Regional security framework.

Unit Assessment

  • 6th Grade: Geography quiz, climate diagram, fact file, resource presentation.
  • 7th Grade: Soviet legacy report, transition evaluation, energy analysis, environmental plan.
  • 8th Grade: Strategy evaluation, development model analysis, cultural report, security framework.
